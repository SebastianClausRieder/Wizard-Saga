Game Instructions

  • Key Instruction
      • A or Left Arrow for Walk left and D or Right Arrow for Walk right.
      If you press and hold ShiftLeft by using A or D the Character will Run.
      W or Up Arrow to interact with Doors or Totems.
      Space to Jump.
      1 to 6 are your skills. "If a skill is grayed out, you cant use it."
      You can perform a combo attack by pressing 4 and 5 in quick succession.
      R to use Red Potion or B to use Blue Potion.
      For Fullscreen press F10. "Only the Game"
      The background music can be controlled with P.
  • Instruction to win the Game
      • Find the two Totems to unlock the final two skills that will help you defeat Medusa.
      Collect all the items you can find.
      Chests you can open with attacks.
      To finish the game you have to collect the star.
